Keep track of your upcoming Color Line cruise directly from Homey. Color Line Booking automatically retrieves your booking details -- departure and arrival times, ship name, cabin information, guest count, price, and return leg data for round trips -- and keeps everything updated so you always know exactly when your next sailing is.

Use it in your flows to trigger automations based on how many hours remain until departure, or react to changes in price, ship assignment, or schedule. Just enter your last name and booking number during setup, and all your booking data is available as live device capabilities and flow tokens throughout Homey.
